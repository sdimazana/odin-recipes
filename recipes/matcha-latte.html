MATCHA LATTE

INGREDIENTS
  • 1 tbsp matcha powder
  • 1 tbsp hot water
  • 1 tbsp sweetened condensed milk
  • 3/4 cup of milk
  • Ice
NOTES
  • Recipes with few ingredients rely more heavily on the quality of the ingredients used. Ceremonial matcha has better quality than culinary matcha and yields better flavor.
  • Honey can be substituted for sweetened condensed milk. Using honey creates a different flavor profile as honey has a more distinct taste compared to sweetened condensed milk. I find that I need less honey to achieve the same sweetness level created with 1 tbsp sweetened condensed milk, but this will vary on the honey. Some types of honey are sweeter than others.
  • Some plant-based milk has more added sugar than regular milk and you may want to reduce the amount of sweetener.
STEPS
  1. Sieve 1 tbsp of matcha powder into a cup.
  2. Add 1 tbsp of hot water to the cup and mix thoroughly using a matcha whisk or milk frother.
  3. Once the mixture is smooth with no lumps, add the sweetened condensed milk.
  4. Add your milk of choice. Mix with a milk frother until fully combined.
NOTES
  • If making a hot matcha latte, heat the milk before adding it to the matcha mixture.
