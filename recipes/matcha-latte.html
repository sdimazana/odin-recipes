Matcha Latte

Description

Refreshing and mildly sweet!

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp of matcha powder
  • 1 tbsp of hot water
  • 1 tbsp sweetened condensed milk
  • 3/4 cup of milk
  • Ice

Steps

  1. Combine hot water and matcha powder in a cup. Mix with a milk frother.
  2. Once the mixture is smooth with no lumps, add the sweetened condensed milk.
  3. Add your milk of choice. Mix with a milk frother till fully combined.
  4. Add ice as desired.
