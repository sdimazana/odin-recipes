MATCHA LATTE

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 tbsp of matcha powder
  • 1 tbsp of hot water
  • 1 tbsp sweetened condensed milk
  • 3/4 cup of milk
  • Ice

STEPS

  1. Combine hot water and matcha powder in a cup. Mix with a milk frother.
  2. Once the mixture is smooth with no lumps, add the sweetened condensed milk.
  3. Add your milk of choice. Mix with a milk frother till fully combined.
  4. Add ice as desired.

TIPS

  • Honey can be substituted for sweetened condensed milk
  • Taste the latte before adding ice to check the sweetness.
    Add more sweetener as desired. After ice is added, it
    becomes more difficult to thoroughly mix the sweetener
    into the latte.
  • If the latte is too sweet, more milk or matcha can be added
    according to your preference. When adding more matcha,
    combine the matcha with an equal amount of hot water and
    thoroughly mix before adding to your latte.
  • For 75% sweetness, use 3/4 tbsp of condensed milk.
    For 50% sweetness, use 1/2 tbsp of condensed milk.
